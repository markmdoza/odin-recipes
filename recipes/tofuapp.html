Tofu Hors D'oeuvres

Pan fried tofu in a crispy rice paper

Tofu, fresh vegetables with fried rice paper, in soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Rice Paper
  • Green Onion
  • Carrots
  • White Turnips
  • Soy Sauce
  • Lime Juice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Paprika
  • Sesame Oil
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Corn Starch

Cooking Instructions

  1. Clean tofu, and pat dry with paper towel - set aside.
  2. Julienne cut the carrots. white turnips, and green onion - set aside.
  3. Use a sauce pot to heat up the soy sauce.
  4. Pour in the rice vinegar, paprika, sesame oil, oyster sauce, and sugar - bring to a boil.
  5. Once the sauce has come to a boil, add in the lime juice, salt, and pepper - set aside to let cool.
  6. Cover the tofu in corn starch and fry it until golden brown - set aside.
  7. Quickly deep fry rice paper with a ladle - set aside.
  8. Mix the sauce with the tofu until it is evenly covered.
  9. Add vegetables to the tofu and sauce mixture.
  10. Evenly distribute the mixture into the crispy rice paper.
  11. Serve hot. Enjoy!
